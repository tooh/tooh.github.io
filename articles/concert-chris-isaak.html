Concert chris isaak

Tue 15 June 2010 Tue 15 June 2010
By Peter Florijn
Category: Music

Chris Isaak¶

I think I’m a fan of Chris Isaak for almost 30 years. Starting with probably his hit Wicked Game. I was never in the opportunity to visit one of his concerts.

On Tuesday 15 June I visited his concert in 013 Tilburg.

Ballads, rock songs, some old work, some recent work, covers from Elvis, Roy Orbison, James Brown. It had it all.

Lots of coverage on You Tube.
